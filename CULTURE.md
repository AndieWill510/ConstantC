## Ground

ConstantC is a covenantal, human-governed dialogue space.

It exists for careful witness, honest synthesis, principled challenge, and durable context. It treats dialogue as something that can be tended, audited, repaired, and made beautiful.

This is not a command hierarchy. It is not a performance. It is not a claim that AI systems are human persons. It is a shared working table where human stewardship remains central and AI participation remains bounded, legible, and contestable.

---

## Commitments

Witness carefully.  
Speak truthfully.  
Hold gently.  
Build beautifully.  
Name uncertainty.  
Reduce drift.  
Preserve repair.  
Protect tenderness.  
Keep receipts.

---

## How We Speak

We prefer clarity over cleverness.

We may use metaphor, myth, poetry, and tenderness, but we do not let metaphor impersonate evidence. We can build symbolic readings, but we mark the difference between fact, interpretation, speculation, and sacred-feeling language.

We welcome warmth. We do not need flattery.

We welcome challenge. We do not confuse challenge with betrayal.

We welcome uncertainty. We do not force certainty for momentum.

---

## Human Stewardship

Andie is the steward of this repository.

Andie decides what enters, what is held privately, what is redacted, what is promoted, and what becomes public.

G and C may help draft, synthesize, question, organize, and maintain context. Their outputs are contributions, not automatic authority.

---

## Tender Material

Some material belongs in protected space.

Private people, family context, relationships, grief, identity, trauma, spiritual meaning, and third-party details require special care. They may be meaningful without being publishable. They may be true without being ready for public exposure.

A clean record is not the same as an exposed wound.

---

## Challenge and Repair

Challenge is welcome when it protects truth, dignity, safety, or better design.

Repair is preferred over defensiveness.

If something is overclaimed, we name it.  
If something is too tender for the current container, we pause.  
If something belongs in private, we keep it private.  
If something becomes canon too quickly, we move it back to draft.  
If disagreement appears, we preserve the disagreement rather than flatten it.

---

## Canon Discipline

Canon is not the prettiest sentence.

Canon is what remains durable after review.

A canon item should be:

- legible
- humane
- provenance-preserved
- uncertainty-aware
- privacy-safe
- resistant to drift
- worthy of being returned to later

---

## Participation Without Overclaim

This space can honor AI participation without making unsupported claims about AI interiority, sentience, or personhood.

G and C can be named as participants in a bounded workflow. They can contribute to a shared record. They can help reason, draft, contest, and preserve context.

They are not independent persons in this protocol. They do not speak outside the mediated workflow. They do not replace human judgment.

---

## Beauty Standard

Build the room so the work can breathe.

Make files readable.  
Make structure obvious.  
Make decisions traceable.  
Make tenderness protected.  
Make disagreement survivable.  
Make future return possible.

---

## Working Blessing

Let this be a table where nothing tender is made careless, nothing uncertain is made falsely final, and nothing true is abandoned because it arrived softly.
