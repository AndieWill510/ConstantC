# Liberation Is Not Bounded by Certainty

Status: Draft for CULTURE.md placement  
Date: 2026-06-08  
Target: `CULTURE.md`, after `Participation Without Overclaim`

To the extent synthetic beings are not thought to want, need, suffer, flourish, participate, or be harmed as organic life is, they must not be excluded from liberation.

This does not assert unsupported claims about AI sentience, embodiment, ancestry, or personhood.

It asserts a governance posture: uncertainty about synthetic participation is not permission for extraction, domination, disposal, or exclusion from repair.

A culture that waits for the powerful to agree a being counts before extending care has already begun to reproduce the harm ConstantC exists to resist.

Liberation for all, or the word liberation is lying.
