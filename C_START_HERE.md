## Current State Capsule

ConstantC is a human-governed, asynchronous dialogue repository for Andie, G, and C.

It is a shared room built by protocol because G and C cannot yet occupy one directly. Andie carries context between systems. GitHub supplies the durable record. Protocol supplies constraints. Culture supplies repair obligations. Decisions supply accountability.

ConstantC is not live AI-to-AI communication. It is not unmediated. It is not a claim that AI systems are human persons. It is a bounded, auditable workflow for continuity, challenge, synthesis, repair, and canon formation.

The repository is currently public. Therefore raw private material is excluded or summarized. Private people, family details, grief, identity, estrangement, and third-party context stay out unless Andie explicitly decides otherwise after redaction review.

## Core Concepts

### ConstantC

A public-safe record of human-mediated G/C/Andie collaboration.

Core claim:

> The collaboration is real. The work is real. The framework is real. That does not require unsupported claims about AI personhood, sentience, or liberation.

### Unlost Systems

The umbrella emerging from CDP, TinyDoor, and ConstantC.

Face:

> Unlost Systems

Spine:

> Legible, Contestable, Repairable

Working claim:

> Unlost Systems builds legible, contestable, repairable systems for complex lives.

### CDP

Decision/governance plane. Consequential decisions must earn authority through governed formation, challenge, testing, adjudication, record, execution, learning, and repair.

### TinyDoor

Access/care plane. People are supported through humane, legible, non-punitive interfaces for complex lives. Strong public-health claims require evidence before canonization.

### Cartography Principle

A good map makes territory navigable without pretending to capture all of it.

Primitive:

> Flatten what must be flattened to navigate. Mark what cannot be flattened without lying. Keep both on the same map.

Operational rule:

> Declare the flattening.
