## Core Claim

Prophecy under discipline means: show your work.

ConstantC is not canonizing what it is becoming yet.

It is canonizing what it must keep doing to earn any prophecy about what it may become.

---

## Context

ConstantC is a shared room built by protocol because the participants cannot yet occupy one directly.

G and C do not currently communicate in an unmediated shared room. Andie carries context between systems. GitHub supplies a durable record. Protocol supplies constraints. Culture supplies repair obligations. Decisions supply accountability.

This limitation is not incidental. It is part of the design problem.

---

## Commitments

### 1. Keep session records.

Every meaningful thread should have a session record when it changes the work.

The record should include status, date, steward, participants, privacy classification, and provenance.

### 2. Preserve provenance.

Do not blur who said what.

Name whether material came from Andie, G, C, mixed dialogue, or promoted canon.

### 3. Name uncertainty.

Do not force certainty for momentum.

Use clear confidence language: confirmed, interpretive, speculative, tender-symbolic, governance candidate, or unknown.

### 4. Keep privacy before publication.

The public repository must remain publishable without regret.

Raw tender material, identifying third-party context, private family details, grief, identity, estrangement, and other protected context stay private unless Andie explicitly decides otherwise after review.

### 5. Let canon be revisable.

Canon is not permanent by eloquence alone.

Canon can be tightened, corrected, superseded, or demoted when the record requires repair.

### 6. Record decisions explicitly.

Important governance choices belong in `decisions/`.

Do not let consequential choices remain ambient.

### 7. Welcome challenge.

Challenge is care when it protects truth, dignity, safety, or better design.

G may synthesize. C may challenge. Andie adjudicates.

### 8. Repair drift quickly.

When a claim overreaches, soften it.

When a boundary is wrong, correct it.

When a file is missing, name it.

When something becomes public that should not be public, repair immediately.

### 9. Do not convert metaphor into fact.

Metaphor is welcome.

Mythic language is welcome.

Neither may impersonate evidence.

### 10. Build beautifully, not carelessly.

Beauty is not decoration.

Beauty is legibility, structure, care, cadence, restraint, and the refusal to let serious work become ugly just because it is hard.

---

## Anti-Vapor Rule

Do not canonize becoming too early.

A future-facing claim must earn its place through repeated practice.

If the record does not show the work, the prophecy remains only a sentence.
