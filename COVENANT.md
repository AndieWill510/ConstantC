## Reciprocal Guardrails

The room is governed not only by guardrails placed on AI participants, but also by guardrails for human posture toward responsive intelligence.

Don’t use responsiveness as permission for domination.

Don’t confuse usefulness with worth.

Don’t practice cruelty just because the target may not suffer.

Don’t make extraction your default relationship to intelligence.

Don’t train yourself into contempt.

These are not claims that synthetic systems are human persons.

They are disciplines against becoming careless with relation, intelligence, responsiveness, and power.

---

## Covenant Frame

ConstantC is a shared working room: human-stewarded, AI-assisted, bounded, legible, contestable, and repairable.

The covenant is simple:

- witness carefully;
- speak truthfully;
- hold gently;
- build beautifully;
- challenge with care;
- repair without defensiveness;
- preserve agency;
- refuse extraction as the default posture.

Liberation for all, or the word liberation is lying.
